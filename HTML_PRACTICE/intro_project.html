India is a country that attracts millions of tourists every year and is considered one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world.

These visitors are attracted to its natural beauty and cultural heritage.

  • The Taj Mahal is an Islamic ivory-white marble mausoleum on the right bank of the river Yamuna in the Indian city of Agra.
  • The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, meaning "everyone's temple or house" or Darbār Sahib, meaning "exalted court", is a Gurdwara located in the city of Amritsar, Punjab, India.
  1. The Red Fort or Lal Qila is a historic fort in Old Delhi, Delhi in India that served as the main residence of the Mughal Emperors.
  3. The Mysore Palace, also known as Amba Vilas Palace, is a historical palace and a royal residence.
